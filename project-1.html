PolyTak

A website that helps with multitasking during school by having some of the most common things you need. Think of it as your backpack, instead of having to bring calculator, dictionary, a clock/timer/stopwatch, a notebook to take note, etc, just use polytask. It increase your productivity and it will help you enjoy and learn efficiently at the same time!

Live Link

Project Overview

How many times does it happend with you that you forget to bring the calculator/notebook/pen etc. with you to the school?well now it's not a problem because polytask got your back!! It comes with various tools which are extreamly useful to students in their day-to-day life.

PolyTask includes multiple features listed below:

  • A scientific calculator
  • A built in Dictionary
  • A timer/stopwatch/clock
  • A notebook for taking notes
  • Snake game, only you're dying of boredom (-_-)
  • A button to add windows
  • A button to delete all windows
  • A button to switch to dark mode/light mode/auto
  • Keybindings
